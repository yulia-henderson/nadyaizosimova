    "Dark night Bright Stars exhibition of decorative paintings consists of 20 works united by one semantic and graphic theme. This is an exhibition about my own experience of encountering a living void, and the consequences of this experience. This project can be considered as an attempt to convey the feeling of being in an empty space, and share what seems important in this experience. This is a set of my images and sensations, emotional and spiritual experience, dressed in color and form."
